About SaniClean

Pioneering hygiene and sustainable sanitation since 2010

Our Journey So Far

Founded in 2010 by environmental engineers and public health advocates, SaniClean was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves a clean and safe environment.

Starting as a small waste collection service in Accra, we have grown into one of Ghana’s most trusted sanitation companies, serving over 850 clients including schools, hospitals, corporate offices, and residential communities.

Today, we combine cutting-edge technology, eco-friendly practices, and highly trained professionals to deliver world-class sanitation solutions.
